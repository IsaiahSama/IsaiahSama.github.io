Discord Bots

What is Discord

Discord is an American VoIP, instant messaging and digital distribution platform designed for creating communities. Users communicate with voice calls, video calls, text messaging, media and files in private chats or as part of communities called "servers."

What is a Discord Bot

Discord bots are AIs that can perform a number of useful automated tasks on your server, such as welcoming new members, moderating content, and banning rule breakers. You can use Discord bot commands to add music, memes, games, and other content to your server.

What do I do

My goal here is to create custom discord bots for servers/users which can serve and handle a wide array of tasks (except music). The only real limit is your imagination (and discord itself :] ). By default, all discord bots that I create will be tailored for a specific server unless otherwise specified.
