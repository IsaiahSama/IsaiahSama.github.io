Discord Bots

What is a Discord Bot

Discord bots are AIs that can perform a number of useful automated tasks on your server, such as welcoming new members, moderating content, and banning rule breakers. You can use Discord bot commands to add activities, memes, games, and other content to your server.

What do I do

My goal here is to create custom discord bots for servers/users which can serve and handle a wide array of tasks (except music). The only real limit is your imagination (and discord itself). By default, all discord bots that I create will be tailored for a specific server unless otherwise specified. Feel free to come to me with any ideas you might have, as I have no problem learning new technologies in order to satisfy your needs.

Take note of the following

  • For this service, a consultation fee of $50 USD is required.
  • The Base Prices listed below are exactly that, base prices. Prices will then scale based on project complexity.
  • A fee of $10 USD will be required per month for bot hosting
  • My bots are designed using evolutionary prototyping. Therefore, I will begin with your request in a basic form, and then continue to develop and improve on it.
  • For bots that are to be designed for more than 1 server, an extra $5 USD will be requested
  • One of my greatest traits is my development time, which is generally very fast.
  • I do not make Music Bots

What Is Software Automation

IT automation, sometimes referred to as infrastructure automation, is the use of software to create repeatable instructions and processes to replace or reduce human interaction with IT systems.
